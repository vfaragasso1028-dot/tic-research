TicScore — CAN SLIM Scoring (100 pts)

G — Growth0–40 pts · Current + annual EPS growth, revenue growth, earnings acceleration
V — Supply/Demand0–20 pts · Float size, relative volume
Q — Quality0–20 pts · ROE ≥17%, profit margins
S — Sponsorship0–20 pts · Institutional ownership, analyst consensus

Pass threshold: ≥50. Exempt sectors: Financials, Real Estate, Energy, Mining, Clinical Biotech, Utilities.

TicStage — Stan Weinstein Stage Analysis

Uses 150-day SMA proxy for the 30-week moving average. Stage 2 (Advancing) is the optimal swing trading environment. PowerStack override: Stage 2 confirmed early when 9EMA > 21EMA > 50SMA above the 150 SMA.
